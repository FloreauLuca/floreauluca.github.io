About Me

I am Floreau Luca, I am 21 years old and I am a French video game developer. I graduated from a Bachelor of Games Programming at the SAE Institute in Geneva.
I am passionate about video games and I want to share my passion. My goal is to create games that can be played by anyone, anywhere.
I created my first game on my own when I was 10 years old on Game Develop.
I develop with Unity 3D and C ++.
If you like what you see, you can check out more of what I do on my twitter and my itch.io .

Projects

AerRacers

2020-2021

G P

AerRacers is a local multi-player pod racing game running on Nintendo Switch.

This project was created in 8 months as part of my Bachelor.
I worked in a team with 4 of my classmates in C++ using the NekoEngine, we also work with 8 tools programmers, 10 visual artists, and 2 audio artists.
I was the Lead Programmer, Producer, Engine Programmer and Gameplay Programmer.
I learned a lot working with a big team on a long-term project.
More information about this project at https://floreauluca.github.io/projects/aerracers

Check out the release : github.com/SAE-Institute-Geneva/AerRacers/releases
