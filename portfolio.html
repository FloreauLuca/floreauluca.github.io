About Me

I am Floreau Luca, I am 20 years old and I am a French video game developer. I am currently in training course at the SAE Institute in Geneva.
I am passionate about video games and I want to share my passion. My goal is to create games that can be played by anyone, anywhere.
I created my first game on my own when I was 10 years old on Game Develop.
I develop with Unity 3D and C ++.
If you like what you see, you can check out more of what I do on my twitter and my itch.io .

School Projects

AerRacers

Game made from September 2020 to May 2021

AerRacers is a local multi-player pod racing game running on Nintendo Switch.

This project was created in 8 months as part of my Bachelor.
I worked in a team with 4 of my classmates in C++ using the NekoEngine, we also work with 8 tools programmers, 10 visual artists, and 2 audio artists.
I was the Lead Programmer, Producer, Engine Programmer and Gameplay Programmer.
I learned a lot working with a big team on a long-term project.
More information about this project at https://floreauluca.github.io/projects/aerracers

Check out the release : github.com/SAE-Institute-Geneva/AerRacers/releases

Minecraft-like

Program made from June to July 2020

Our Minecraft-like is a project where you can move and construct inside an infinite generated world.

This project was created in 2 months as part of the module of computer graphics.
I worked in a team with 7 of my classmates in C++ using the NekoEngine.
The NekoEngine is a custom C++ Engine using SDL 2.0 and OpenGL ES 3.0.
I enjoyed working on chunk generation and chunk rendering.
More information about this project at https://floreauluca.github.io/projects/minecraft-like

Check out the release : github.com/LoshkinOleg/NekoEngine/releases

Potion Cooking

Game made on November 2018

Potion Cooking is a point'n'click in which you play as a mage who must make and sell magic potions to his customers.

This project was the first game created during my training at SAE.
It allowed me to put into practice what I was learning and to create my first mobile game.
Furthermore, I enjoyed creating the sprites with this minimalist style.
More information about this project at https://floreauluca.github.io/projects/potion-cooking
